Servers & Experience

I've worked for multiple servers in the past, ranging from thousands of players to smaller communities. I usually duo Helper and Builder/Developer!

Best Experiences

  • Hive (Java Edition) (~8000 players): Had a great time meeting with the amazing community and working with them as a Moderator. It was such a good time. I wish Java hadn't shut down.
  • GandziHub (5-10 players): Found through Minehut jobs. I became the head admin and developer. I coded about 80% of the server along with its plugins, settings, TAB, etc. It turned out very professional!
  • Random Smaller Gens (5-30 players): These honestly gave me some of the best times of my life meeting so many people. Truly enjoyed my time there across all sorts of ranks.

Other Experiences

  • Yedupe (5-40 players): A strict server where I accidentally broke a rule regarding staff chat triggers on my first day. I was later unfairly demoted due to a misunderstanding while playing with a teammate. Mostly fair, but strict.
  • Dupestrike (5-25 players): Highly corrupt environment. I spent hours building and moderating, only to be fired and insulted by a rage-baiting owner after dealing with a spammer. Heavily biased staff structure.
